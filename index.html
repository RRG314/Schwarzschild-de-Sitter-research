Evidence Policy

  • Exact: derivation-level statements within the stated model.
  • Empirical: numerical or computational outcomes.
  • Approximation-based: method-dependent results.
  • Disproven / Retracted: retained for provenance.
  • Open: unresolved questions.
Editorial principle: no overclaiming; negative results remain available; model scope is explicit.
