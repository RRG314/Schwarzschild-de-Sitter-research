# SDS Final Report

## What This Repo Now Is

A theorem-first Schwarzschild-de Sitter research repository with exact thermodynamic structure, dimensional closure classification, spectral/QNM cleanup with explicit negative-result retention, a separated tools layer, and a research-program layer that distinguishes proved, validated, conditional, disproved, and open material.

## Strongest Surviving Results

### Proved
- 4D exact entropy identity
- exact temperature-ratio and Carnot-deficit formulas on the fixed-`Lambda` family
- exact monotonic phase-family structure in the `x` parameter
- exact 5D Pythagorean-style closure

### Validated / falsification-backed
- D>=6 two-root fixed-`Lambda` candidate closures fail on explicit witness families
- multiple spectral pattern claims remain demoted or negative rather than overstated

## Main Honest Limits

- most exact thermodynamic structure should be treated as structural consequences of SdS thermodynamics unless literature review proves stronger distinctness
- Eisenstein packaging is useful, but theorem-level novelty must be earned, not assumed
- spectral/QNM lanes remain mixed exact/approximation/negative territory and must stay explicitly labeled

## Best Next Targets

1. strongest literature-distinct consequence of the fixed-`Lambda` exact phase family
2. exact dimensional rigidity beyond the D=4/D=5 isolated closures
3. sharpened no-go theorems for universal closure claims
4. further spectral cleanup with preference for exact negative results over suggestive patterns
